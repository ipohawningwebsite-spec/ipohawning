ABOUT US

Get To Know Us!

Ipoh Awning Sdn. Bhd. is a company that provides fabrication and installation services for awnings, grills, gates, and various other metal products for residential and industrial premises around Ipoh. We prioritize quality at every stage from material selection and manufacturing processes to final installation to ensure strong, durable, and neat results. Each design can be customized according to the customer’s needs and preferences, offered at reasonable prices with value that matches the quality delivered. Our team consists of skilled professionals who are efficient, responsive, and customer-friendly. Our commitment is to provide a smooth, responsive, and satisfying service experience from the initial consultation until the completion of every project.
